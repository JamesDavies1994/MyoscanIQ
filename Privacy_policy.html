# Privacy Policy for MyoScan IQ **Last Updated:** June 29, 2026 ## Introduction MyoScan IQ ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our data practices for our mobile application, a professional Cardiac MRI reference tool made by radiographers, for radiographers. ## Information We Collect MyoScan IQ does not collect, store, or transmit any user data to our servers. Specifically: - No user account or registration is required - No personal information is collected or transmitted to us - No camera or photo library access is used - No tracking, analytics, or telemetry data is collected - No cookies or similar technologies are used ## Local Data Storage All app content and user-created data is stored exclusively on your device. The app includes: - Pre-loaded educational reference images and videos - Static protocol and anatomical information - No cloud storage or external data transmission - All content is available offline ### User-Created Logbook Data The CMR Logbook feature allows you to record details of cardiac MRI examinations you have participated in. This data — including date, scanner manufacturer, field strength, exam type, clinical indication, sequences performed, your role, and any issues encountered — is stored locally on your device using on-device storage. This data is never transmitted to MyoScan IQ or any third party. **Important:** If the app is deleted from your device, all logbook data will be permanently lost. We recommend exporting your logbook regularly. **Professional responsibility:** The logbook is intended for recording your own professional development activities. Do not enter patient-identifiable information into the logbook. ## Data Export The app provides the ability to export your CMR Logbook entries as a CSV file. When you use this feature: - A temporary CSV file is created in your device's local cache - The file is presented via your device's native share sheet, allowing you to save it to your files, email it, or share it with another app of your choice - MyoScan IQ has no visibility into, and does not record, what you do with the exported file - The exported file is not transmitted to MyoScan IQ servers at any point You are solely responsible for the storage and security of any exported files. ## Third-Party Services MyoScan IQ does not integrate with any third-party analytics, tracking, advertising, or backend services. The app functions entirely offline once downloaded. ## Data Sharing and Disclosure We do not collect any user data, and therefore have no data to share, sell, trade, or disclose to any third party. Data you create within the app remains on your device unless you choose to export and share it yourself. ## Data Retention and Deletion All user-created data (such as logbook entries) is stored on your device only. You may delete individual logbook entries at any time within the app. Uninstalling the app will permanently remove all locally stored data. We do not retain any copies of your data. ## Children's Privacy MyoScan IQ is intended for use by medical professionals and students. Since we do not collect any personal information, no data from any user, including children, is gathered or transmitted. ## Changes to This Privacy Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. ## Contact Us If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: **Email**: support@myoscaniq.com ## Consent By using MyoScan IQ, you consent to this Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.
